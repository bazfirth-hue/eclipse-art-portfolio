The Artist

Born Under
a Dark Sky

I'm James, a spray paint artist inspired by space, planets, and the vast unknown. Using bold techniques and layered textures, I create vivid cosmic scenes that explore colour, movement, and atmosphere as my style continues to evolve.

My work is deeply personal. Art has helped me stay grounded after moving away from close friends, offering comfort and perspective through the exploration of something much bigger than myself, bringing a piece of the unknown closer to home.
